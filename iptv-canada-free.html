The Problem with Free IPTV

Completely free IPTV that covers everything (NHL, NFL, NBA, Premier League, UFC) doesn't exist legally. When you find sites claiming "free IPTV Canada" with full sports coverage, they are streaming content without broadcast rights — this is technically illegal in Canada, though enforcement against individual viewers is extremely rare.

The practical problem with free IPTV sites is quality: they buffer at critical moments (Stanley Cup playoffs, Grey Cup), go down without warning, and provide no support when they fail. Missing the final minutes of a Leafs playoff game because your free stream crashed is why most serious sports fans move to paid services.
