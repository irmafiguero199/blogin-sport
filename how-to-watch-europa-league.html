Europa League vs Champions League — What's the Difference?

Both are UEFA club competitions but different tiers:

  • UEFA Champions League — Top clubs from each league. 36 teams in the league phase. Bigger prize money, bigger names.
  • UEFA Europa League — Second-tier competition. 36 teams. Clubs that finish 5th–7th in top leagues, plus Champions League group-stage dropouts. Still top quality football.
  • UEFA Conference League — Third tier. Clubs from smaller leagues and lower-ranked finishers from major leagues.
