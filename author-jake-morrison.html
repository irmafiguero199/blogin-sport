Review Methodology

Every streaming service reviewed on LiveSportStream is tested using a consistent methodology. Jake does not accept payment from services in exchange for reviews. Affiliate relationships are disclosed clearly.

  • 30-day minimum testing period — services are tested over a full month, not just at setup
  • Live game day testing — quality and uptime tested during actual NHL, NFL, Premier League and UFC events
  • Multiple devices — tested on Firestick, Smart TV, iPhone, Android, and laptop simultaneously
  • Speed and buffering measurement — stream quality logged during peak viewing times
  • Channel accuracy verification — every claimed channel is individually verified
  • Customer support testing — support response times and quality measured on multiple occasions
  • Billing transparency check — pricing, cancellation terms, and payment methods verified
