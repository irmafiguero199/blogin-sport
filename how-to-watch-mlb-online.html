Can I watch MLB games for free legally?
Free legal options are limited. CBC occasionally broadcasts Blue Jays games in Canada. In the US, the Fox Sports App is free with a cable login. Most fully free streaming sites operate in a legal gray area — use a VPN for privacy.
How do I avoid MLB blackouts?
Use a reliable VPN (NordVPN, ExpressVPN, or Surfshark) set to a server outside the blackout region. This works with MLB.TV and most streaming services. Connect before opening the streaming app.
