Can I watch The Masters for free?
Yes — The Masters website and app provide free featured group streams during all four rounds. CBS also offers a free live stream of their weekend broadcast on Masters.com. This is the most generous free streaming of any major championship.

NorthStream IPTV at $12.99/month covers every PGA Tour event plus all four majors. If you prefer official services, you'd need ESPN+ ($10.99) for featured groups and Peacock ($7.99) for Golf Channel coverage — totaling $19/month.
How do I watch featured groups coverage?
ESPN+ is the exclusive home of PGA Tour Live featured groups — following specific players through their entire round. IPTV services also carry these feeds through Golf Channel and international broadcasts.
