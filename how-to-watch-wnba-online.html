Frequently Asked Questions

Can I watch WNBA games for free?
Yes — several options exist. ION Television broadcasts 15+ Friday night games free over-the-air. Amazon Prime members get 21 exclusive games. Free streaming sites like SportsSurge carry all games. The WNBA app also offers select free games throughout the season.
What's the cheapest way to watch every WNBA game?
NorthStream IPTV at $12.99/month covers every game with no blackouts. For official services, combine WNBA League Pass ($34.99/year) with an over-the-air antenna for ION/CBS games — but you'll still miss some national broadcasts without cable.
How do I watch WNBA blackout games?
Use a VPN (NordVPN, ExpressVPN) connected to a server outside the blackout region before opening WNBA League Pass. Alternatively, use free streaming sites or IPTV which have no blackout restrictions.
Can I watch WNBA on my phone or Firestick?
Yes — WNBA League Pass and Amazon Prime Video have excellent mobile apps and Firestick apps. Free sites work in mobile browsers. IPTV services provide APK files for Firestick and Android TV with dedicated WNBA channels.
Is WNBA League Pass worth it in 2026?
At $34.99 for the full season (less than $1 per game), League Pass is excellent value for fans who follow one team closely. The Premium tier at $49.99 adds multi-game viewing and no commercials. Note that national TV games are blacked out.
