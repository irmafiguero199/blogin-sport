How to Buy Official Tickets

  • FIFA.com/tickets — The ONLY official source. All sales go through FIFA's ticketing portal. Create a FIFA account to register for ballots.
  • Ballot system — High-demand matches (Final, Semi-Finals, games involving USA/Canada/Mexico/Brazil/Argentina) use random ballot. Apply in the ballot period, winners are randomly selected.
  • First-come-first-served — Less popular group stage matches may have direct sale phases. Check FIFA.com for dates.
  • Hospitality packages — MATCH Hospitality is FIFA's official hospitality partner. Packages include tickets + premium seating, catering, and experiences.

Secondary Market Tickets

  • StubHub — Largest secondary market. Prices significantly above face value for knockout rounds.
  • Viagogo — International secondary market with global inventory.
  • SeatGeek — USA-focused resale platform with price analytics.
  • Warning: FIFA does not authorize secondary sales. Resold tickets may be cancelled at entry. Only buy from verified sellers with buyer guarantees.
