Canadian Stadiums — Toronto & Vancouver

BMO Field (Toronto) — Canada's soccer-specific stadium in downtown Toronto. Located near the CN Tower and Rogers Centre. 7 group stage matches expected. Toronto FC's home ground, expanded for World Cup capacity.

BC Place (Vancouver) — Canada's largest covered stadium with retractable roof. 7 group stage matches. Located in downtown Vancouver with spectacular mountain backdrop. Canada's west coast World Cup venue.

Where is the World Cup 2026 Final stadium?+
The 2026 World Cup Final is at MetLife Stadium in East Rutherford, New Jersey — part of the New York metro area. Capacity: 82,500. The stadium is home to the NFL's New York Giants and New York Jets. The Final is on July 19, 2026.
Which Canadian city hosts World Cup 2026?+
Two Canadian cities host World Cup 2026 matches: Toronto (BMO Field, 7 matches) and Vancouver (BC Place, 7 matches). Canada is a co-host alongside the USA and Mexico — the first time Canada has hosted World Cup matches since 2015 Women's World Cup.
