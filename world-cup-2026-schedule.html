Group Stage Format

World Cup 2026 has 12 groups of 4 teams — the first expanded format in World Cup history. Each group plays 3 matchdays. The top 2 teams from each group advance automatically (24 teams). The 8 best third-place finishers also advance — giving 32 teams in the Round of 32.

The 2026 FIFA World Cup Final is on Sunday, July 19, 2026 at MetLife Stadium in East Rutherford, New Jersey (New York metro area). The stadium holds 82,500 fans. The match will be broadcast live on CTV and TSN in Canada, Fox and Telemundo in the USA.
How many games in World Cup 2026?+
World Cup 2026 has 104 matches total — the most in World Cup history. This is due to the expanded 48-team format introduced in 2026. Previous World Cups had 64 matches (32 teams). The extra matches come from the new Round of 32 phase between the group stage and Round of 16.
